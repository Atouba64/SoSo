Features Built for Trust

We've combined the world's best community savings practices with cutting-edge algorithmic security.

Advanced Rotation Modes

Every community is different. Choose the rotation style that fits your group's financial goals.

The Bidding Engine

Need cash now? Members bid for the pot based on liquidity needs. The interest paid is distributed as a dividend to the rest of the group, turning savings into high-yield investments.

Guarantor Mode

Group creators can receive the first payout fee-free by staking collateral and acting as the group's personal liquidity buffer.

Lottery & Social Mode

For groups focused on absolute fairness and gamification, a provably fair algorithm picks the winner each cycle. Set "Goal Pots" to purchase specific items.

Emergency Solidarity Fund
Active

Group members contribute a small extra fee to a shared digital vault, ready to be disbursed if any member faces an unexpected crisis.
