How SoSo Works

The power of a community savings club, secured by algorithms.

1

Create or Join a Group

Start a group and invite your friends and family from anywhere in the world. Set the cycle length (weekly, monthly) and the contribution amount. Everyone goes through our secure identity verification (KYC) to ensure the community is safe.

2

Automatic Contributions

Link your bank account, card, or Mobile Money wallet. SoSo automatically deducts the agreed amount when the cycle starts. No more chasing people down for cash or sending manual wire transfers.

3

The Smart Payout

Depending on your group's chosen mode (Bidding, Lottery, or Fixed Schedule), the algorithm determines the receiver. The pooled funds are instantly transferred from the secure smart escrow directly to the winner's account.

4

Liquidity Buffer & Credit Building

If someone's payment fails, SoSo's Liquidity Pool covers it instantly, so the receiver gets their full pot on time. The late member settles with SoSo directly. As you successfully complete rotations, we report your positive behavior to credit bureaus, building your formal credit score.
