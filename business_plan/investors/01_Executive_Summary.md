# SoSo: The Digital Likelemba

## Executive Summary

**The Problem:** Traditional banking is inaccessible, expensive, or culturally alienating for billions in emerging markets and their global diasporas. As a result, communities rely on Rotating Savings and Credit Associations (ROSCAs)—known as Likelemba, Tontine, Susu, or Stokvel. These systems move hundreds of billions of dollars annually, but they are geographically restricted, scale-limited, and vulnerable to human fraud because they rely entirely on analog, interpersonal trust.

**The Solution:** SoSo (meaning "Chicken" in Lingala—a symbol of community, steady growth, and living savings) digitizes the ROSCA. We replace analog trust with an algorithmic trust engine, utilizing open banking, credit scoring, stablecoins, and automated smart contracts. 

**The Value Proposition:** SoSo allows users to create, manage, and join global savings rotations with zero risk of organizer fraud, automated late-payment liquidity buffering, and protection against local currency devaluation. We bring the familiarity of the Likelemba to the scale of global fintech.

**Target Market:** 
1. The African Diaspora (sending $100B+ in remittances annually, highly banked but culturally aligned to ROSCAs).
2. The African Continent (massive mobile money penetration, high inflation, reliant on informal credit).

**The Ask:** We are raising a pre-seed round to build the MVP, secure necessary financial licenses/partnerships in our beachhead market (UK/EU Diaspora & Francophone Africa), and launch our private beta.
